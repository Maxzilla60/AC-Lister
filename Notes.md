## Problematic Names
The following villager names can cause certain problems in the application

Accented letters:
* Renée
* O'Hare
* Étoile
* Viché

Duplicates:
* Petunia
* Carmen
* Lulu

## Missing Icons
The following villager names do not have respective small villager icons, most of them are Amiibo villagers

* Cece
* Epona
* Ganon
* Holden
* Inkwell
* Medli
* Viché
* Wolf Link
